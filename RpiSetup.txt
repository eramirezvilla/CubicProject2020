Rpi Setup Steps

Working from your laptop
Download the Balena Etcher application on your laptop
https://www.balena.io/etcher/
Download a zip file of the Raspbian Buster with desktop and recommended software
https://www.raspberrypi.org/downloads/raspbian/
Insert your blank micro sd card into your computer (you may need an adapter)
Open the Balena Etcher application  
For image you want the Raspbian zip file just downloaded 
For target you want your micro sd card (should connect automatically)
Then flash, this will take a while 
Once that is finished, insert the sd card into the powered off rpi 
Then power on your rpi 


Working from your Raspberry Pi
Next you are going to want the latest version of gcc to compile all c/c++ code
Follow the instructions on this website to do so
https://solarianprogrammer.com/2017/12/08/raspberry-pi-raspbian-install-gcc-compile-cpp-17-programs/
To compile C/C++ code:
g++ -std=c++17 -Wall -pedantic -o exampleFile exampleFile.c
To run:
./exampleFile
If you want to download python3 since the GUI may be in python, enter the following commands
https://medium.com/@isma3il/install-python-3-6-or-3-7-and-pip-on-raspberry-pi-85e657aadb1e
Change file names to latest version
Change all mentions of 3.6.5 or whatever to 3.8.2 or latest version
